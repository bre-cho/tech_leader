## Key production rules

- Every 2-3 shots needs a micro hook: face, movement, light change, crowd reaction, or detail.
- Every 4-6 shots needs face close-up or eye contact.
- Avoid long HIGH-HIGH-HIGH energy runs.
- Runway loop should cycle: wide walk → medium walk → beauty close-up → accessory macro → audience reaction → flash camera → hero pose.
- Camera language should breathe: wide → medium → close → macro → release wide.
